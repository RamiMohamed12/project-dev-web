Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about finding and applying for internships

How do I find the right internship for my career goals?

Use our advanced search filters to narrow down opportunities by industry, location, and duration. We recommend focusing on internships that align with your major and desired career path. Our skill-matching algorithm can also suggest internships based on your profile and experience.

Are the internships on Navigui paid or unpaid?

We list both paid and unpaid internships, but each listing clearly indicates compensation details. You can filter your search to show only paid opportunities. We advocate for fair compensation and encourage companies to offer paid positions that value your skills and time.

How can I make my application stand out to employers?

Complete your profile with relevant skills and coursework, upload a tailored resume for each application, and write thoughtful answers to application questions. Our Resume Builder tool can help optimize your resume with industry keywords, and our Application Coach provides personalized feedback to improve your chances.

Can I apply for remote internships in other countries?

Yes! Many companies on our platform offer remote internships that you can pursue from anywhere. Keep in mind potential time zone differences and work permit requirements for international opportunities. Use our location filter to find remote positions or browse international opportunities that match your skills.

How long does the application process typically take?

The timeline varies by company, but most employers respond within 2-3 weeks of application. Our Application Tracking feature shows real-time status updates and notifies you of any changes. Some companies conduct multiple interview rounds, which can extend the process to 4-6 weeks from initial application to offer.
